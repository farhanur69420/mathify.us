Essential Formulas

Structural Engineering
Bending Stress
σ=M⋅yI

Bending stress at distance y from the neutral axis.

Fluid Mechanics
Bernoulli's Equation
P+12ρv2+ρgh=const

Energy conservation along a streamline in ideal fluid flow.

Mathematics
Euler's Identity
eiπ+1=0

The most beautiful equation — connecting five fundamental constants.

Structural Engineering
Euler's Buckling Load
Pcr=π2EI(KL)2

Critical compressive load at which a slender column will buckle.

Mathematics
Taylor Series
f(x)=∑n=0∞f(n)(a)n!(x−a)n

Approximating any smooth function as an infinite polynomial.

Geotechnical
Terzaghi Bearing Capacity
qu=cNc+qNq+12γBNγ

Ultimate bearing capacity of shallow foundations in soil.

Reinforced Concrete (WSD)
Cracked Section k & j
k=(ρn)2+2ρn−ρn j=1−k/3

Neutral axis depth ratio k and moment arm ratio j for a cracked RC section under WSD. ρ = As/(bd), n = Es/Ec.
