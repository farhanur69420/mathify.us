Essential Formulas

Structural Engineering
Bending Stress
σ = M · y / I

The bending stress in a beam at distance y from the neutral axis, where M is the bending moment and I is the second moment of area.

Fluid Mechanics
Bernoulli's Equation
P + ½ρv² + ρgh = const

Energy conservation along a streamline — relates pressure, velocity, and elevation in ideal fluid flow.

Mathematics
Euler's Identity
e^(iπ) + 1 = 0

Often called the most beautiful equation in mathematics — connecting five fundamental constants in one elegant expression.

Structural Engineering
Euler's Buckling Load
P_cr = π²EI / (KL)²

The critical compressive load at which a slender column will buckle, depending on its stiffness EI and effective length KL.

Mathematics
Taylor Series
f(x) = Σ fⁿ(a)/n! · (x-a)ⁿ

Approximating any smooth function as an infinite polynomial — the foundation of numerical methods in engineering.

Geotechnical
Terzaghi Bearing Capacity
q_u = cNc + qNq + ½γBNγ

The ultimate bearing capacity of shallow foundations in soil, combining cohesion, surcharge, and soil weight contributions.
