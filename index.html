Essential Formulas

Structural Engineering
Bending Stress
\( \sigma = \dfrac{M \cdot y}{I} \)

Bending stress at distance y from the neutral axis, where M is the bending moment and I is the second moment of area.

Fluid Mechanics
Bernoulli's Equation
\( P + \tfrac{1}{2}\rho v^2 + \rho g h = \text{const} \)

Energy conservation along a streamline — relates pressure, velocity, and elevation in ideal fluid flow.

Mathematics
Euler's Identity
\( e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0 \)

The most beautiful equation in mathematics — connecting five fundamental constants in one elegant expression.

Structural Engineering
Euler's Buckling Load
\( P_{cr} = \dfrac{\pi^2 EI}{(KL)^2} \)

The critical compressive load at which a slender column will buckle, depending on stiffness EI and effective length KL.

Mathematics
Taylor Series
\( f(x) = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \dfrac{f^{(n)}(a)}{n!}(x-a)^n \)

Approximating any smooth function as an infinite polynomial — the foundation of numerical methods in engineering.

Geotechnical
Terzaghi Bearing Capacity
\( q_u = cN_c + qN_q + \tfrac{1}{2}\gamma B N_\gamma \)

The ultimate bearing capacity of shallow foundations in soil, combining cohesion, surcharge, and soil weight contributions.

Reinforced Concrete (RC Design)
Cracked Section k & j
\( k = \sqrt{(\rho n)^2 + 2\rho n} - \rho n \) \( j = 1 - k/3 \)

Neutral axis depth ratio k and moment arm ratio j for a cracked RC section under Working Stress Design. ρ = As/(bd), n = Es/Ec.
