Symmetry & Tessellation

Discover lines of symmetry in shapes, create symmetrical patterns, and explore how shapes tile a plane without gaps (tessellation) — the math behind Islamic art, honeycombs, and floor tiles!

Lines of Symmetry

A shape has a line of symmetry if you can fold it along that line and both halves match exactly. Some shapes have many lines of symmetry!

Square
4 lines of symmetry
Rectangle
2 lines of symmetry
Equilateral △
3 lines of symmetry
Circle
Infinite lines!

Draw a Symmetric Pattern

Click cells on the LEFT half — the right half is automatically reflected to create a symmetric pattern!

Tessellation Explorer

Tessellation is when shapes tile a surface with no gaps or overlaps. Squares, equilateral triangles, and regular hexagons all tessellate perfectly.
