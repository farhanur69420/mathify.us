Factors & Multiples

Understand what factors and multiples are, find all factors of a number, identify prime and composite numbers, and discover HCF and LCM using interactive tools.

What Are Factors?

A factor of a number divides it exactly with no remainder. For example, the factors of 12 are 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, and 12.

Enter a number below to find all its factors.

What Are Multiples?

A multiple of a number is what you get when you multiply it by 1, 2, 3, 4, … The first 5 multiples of 6 are: 6, 12, 18, 24, 30.

Prime Numbers — Sieve of Eratosthenes

HCF & LCM Calculator

HCF (Highest Common Factor) is the largest number that divides both numbers. LCM (Lowest Common Multiple) is the smallest number that both numbers divide into.

Word Problem — Bar Model

Aisha has 24 red beads and 36 blue beads. She wants to arrange them in equal groups with no beads left over, using the largest possible group size. How many groups will there be, and how many beads in each group?

1
Find HCF(24, 36): Factors of 24 = {1,2,3,4,6,8,12,24}, Factors of 36 = {1,2,3,4,6,9,12,18,36}
2
Common factors = {1,2,3,4,6,12}, so HCF = 12
3
Groups of red = 24÷12 = 2, Groups of blue = 36÷12 = 3. Total = 5 groups of 12
