Fraction ↔ Decimal Converter

To convert a fraction to a decimal, divide the numerator by the denominator. Common ones to memorise: 1/2 = 0.5, 1/4 = 0.25, 3/4 = 0.75, 1/5 = 0.2.

Adding & Subtracting Decimals

Line up the decimal points! Then add or subtract digit by digit, just like whole numbers.

Word Problem — Bar Model

A bottle holds 1.5 litres of water. Rohan drinks 0.75 litres. His sister drinks 0.4 litres. How much water is left in the bottle?

1
Total drunk = 0.75 + 0.40 = 1.15 litres
2
Water left = 1.50 − 1.15 = 0.35 litres
