Mixed Numbers & Fractions

Learn to convert between improper fractions and mixed numbers, compare fractions with different denominators, and add or subtract fractions using equivalent fractions.

Improper Fractions & Mixed Numbers

An improper fraction has a numerator larger than or equal to the denominator (e.g., 7/3). A mixed number combines a whole number and a proper fraction (e.g., 2⅓).

Denominator
7/3 = 2 remainder 1 → Mixed number: 2 and 1/3

Word Problem — Bar Model

A rope is 3 and 2/5 metres long. Another rope is 1 and 4/5 metres long. What is the total length? What is the difference in their lengths?

1
Convert: 3²/₅ = 17/5, and 1⁴/₅ = 9/5
2
Total = 17/5 + 9/5 = 26/5 = 5 and 1/5 metres
3
Difference = 17/5 − 9/5 = 8/5 = 1 and 3/5 metres
