Angles in Shapes

The sum of angles inside any triangle is always 180°. The sum of angles inside any quadrilateral is always 360°.

Find the missing angle in a triangle:

° ° + ? = 180°

Find missing angle in a quadrilateral:

Word Problem

In triangle PQR, angle P = 55° and angle Q = 75°. What is the measure of angle R? What type of triangle is it?

1
Sum of angles in a triangle = 180°
2
Angle R = 180° − 55° − 75° = 50°
3
All angles < 90°, so it's an acute triangle
