Word Problems

At a school sports day, students stand in 8 rows with 9 students in each row. After the event, 15 students leave early. How many students are still at the sports day?

1
Find total students: 8 × 9 = 72 students
2
Subtract those who left: 72 − 15 = 57 students
3
Answer: 57 students are still at the sports day.

⭐ Challenge Problem

Ravi buys 5 packets of biscuits. Each packet has 8 biscuits. He shares them equally among 4 friends. How many biscuits does each friend get?

1
Total biscuits: 5 × 8 = 40 biscuits
2
Share equally: 40 ÷ 4 = 10 biscuits each
