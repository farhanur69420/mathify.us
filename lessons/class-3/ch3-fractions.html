Word Problem with Bar Model

🍕 Maria and John share a pizza. Maria ate 3/8 of the pizza. John ate 2/8 of the pizza. How much pizza did they eat altogether? How much is left?

1
Add the fractions: 3/8 + 2/8 = 5/8 (same denominator, just add numerators!)
2
How much left? 8/8 − 5/8 = 3/8 of the pizza remains.
3
Answer: They ate 5/8 altogether. 3/8 is left.
