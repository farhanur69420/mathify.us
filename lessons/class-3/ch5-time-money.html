Word Problem — Bar Model

Ravi went to the market with ₹500. He bought a notebook for ₹45, a pen for ₹18, and a lunch box for ₹120. How much money did he spend in total? How much money did he have left?

1
Total spent = ₹45 + ₹18 + ₹120 = ₹183
2
Money left = ₹500 − ₹183 = ₹317

Ravi spent ₹183 and had ₹317 left.
