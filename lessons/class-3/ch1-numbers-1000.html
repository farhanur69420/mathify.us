Word Problem with Bar Model

Siti collects stickers. She has 347 animal stickers and 215 flower stickers. Her brother Ahmad has 89 fewer stickers than Siti's total. How many stickers does Ahmad have?

Singapore Bar Model — shows the relationship between quantities
1
Find Siti's total: 347 + 215 = 562 stickers
2
Ahmad has 89 fewer: 562 − 89 = 473 stickers
3
Answer: Ahmad has 473 stickers.
