What is a Bar Model?

A bar model is a picture that helps you "see" a math problem before solving it. Used in Singapore schools since the 1980s — it's why Singapore ranks #1 in global math tests.

"Sarah has 3 times as many stickers as Tom. Together they have 48 stickers. How many does Sarah have?"

The bar model below makes this instantly clear — no algebra needed!
